Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

Overview

Varied Veggie is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Data storage

All data you create in Varied Veggie - your meal history, ingredient preferences, and settings - is stored in two places:

  • Locally on your device
  • In your private iCloud container (if you are signed into iCloud)

iCloud sync uses Apple's CloudKit framework. Your data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Data we collect

None. Varied Veggie does not collect analytics, usage data, crash reports, or any other information. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, and no tracking.

Third parties

Varied Veggie does not share data with third parties. There are no ads, no analytics SDKs, and no external services beyond Apple's iCloud.

Deleting your data

You can delete your meal history at any time within the app. Uninstalling the app removes all local data. To remove iCloud data, go to Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage on your device.
