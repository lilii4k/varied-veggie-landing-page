VariedVeggie

Break out of your meal rut.

VariedVeggie tracks what you've cooked and surfaces the ingredients you haven't used in a while. Pick a protein, a vegetable, and a carb — and get recipe ideas that actually make sense together.

Features

Variety-first sorting
Ingredients you haven't eaten recently appear at the top. The ones you had yesterday sink to the bottom.
Cuisine matching
Proteins, vegetables, and carbs are grouped by cuisine so your combinations make sense.
Recipes & prep ideas
Get suggestions for how to cook your chosen combo, or pick your own prep methods.
Surprise Me
Tap the dice for a random cuisine-matched meal when you can't decide.
iCloud sync
Your meal history carries over to new devices automatically.
Vegan mode
Filter to plant-based proteins with a single toggle.

Privacy

No account required. No ads. No tracking. Your data stays on your device and in your private iCloud container.
